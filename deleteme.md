What does creating a dataset *do*?

Example: After a dinner party I am left with the dirty dishes. 

To create a dataset, I count the number of dishes washed, the duration of the dishwashing session, the amount of soap and water used, drying time. 

Now what do I DO with that data? 

I could have just looked at the dirty dishes and realized that I ought to wash them. 

What was the point of that exercise? 

Unless I operate at a scale (think hotel chain) where potential savings on the cost of the soap makes a meaningful difference in my profits, it's waste of time and effort.
