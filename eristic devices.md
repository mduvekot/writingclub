"**The suggestion that one cannot prove that LLMs do not have intentionality or consciousness is a rhetorical tool. It is used by unfaithful interlocutors to convince others to concede, repeatedly, the possibility that LLMs in fact possess them**. Its effect is not to increase the rigor of experimental design, but to stymie conversation and inquiry that resist it." [Vincent Charchidi](https://vincentcarchidi.substack.com/p/against-false-indulgences-in-llm)

What kind of tool is "the suggestion that one cannot prove that LLMs do not have intentionality or consciousness"?

It is like "the suggestion that one cannot disprove that between the Earth and Mars there is a china teapot revolving about the sun in an elliptical orbit"?

The input is a claim: "You cannot prove that LLMs have consciousness", which implies—since there is no plausible mechanism by which this might happen— that it is reasonable to assume that LLMs do not have consciousness. Then we take that claim and invert it and make the counter-claim: "You cannot **dis**prove that LLMs have consciousness". (Note that we don't argue that we **can** prove that LLMs have consciousness) and suggest that that inversion has also inverted the implication of the first claim. So we're saying that 𝐴→𝐵 therefore¬𝐴→¬𝐵 (inversion). But that is invalid, the inverse's truth value is not at all dependent on whether or not the original proposition was true. We could have said.  𝐴→𝐵 is equivalent to ¬𝐵→¬𝐴 (contraposition)  which, in plain English, using our first claim means something like: **“If it is** _**not**_ **reasonable to assume LLMs are not conscious, then there must be some plausible mechanism by which LLMs** _**could**_ **be conscious.”** Which, sure, seems reasonable enough.

So the tool's function is to take a true claim and turn it into a false claim by using a rule of inference incorrectly, under the assumption that it won't be noticed by most people (who are very likely not fluent in propositional calculus).  **"The suggestion that one cannot prove that LLMs do not have intentionality or consciousness" is an eristic device, it's purpose is to win an argument by whatever means, truth and logic be damned).**
