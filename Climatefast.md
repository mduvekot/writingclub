### Where to look

- **Ontario Ministry of the Environment, Conservation and Parks (MECP)**
    
    - The MECP issues ECAs for any facility discharging industrial wastewater.
        
    - These approvals are searchable through the Environmental Compliance Approval registry on Ontario.ca..
        
- **Environmental Registry of Ontario (ERO)**
    
    - Public notices of new or amended ECAs are posted here for comment.
        
    - Searching “Microsoft YTO40” or “Hydro One data center” may show filings related to water discharge.
        
- **Municipal sewer use permits**
    
    - Blowdown water is typically discharged into the municipal sanitary sewer system.
        
    - The City of Toronto (or York Region, depending on the exact siting) enforces sewer use bylaws that set limits on temperature, pH, and chemical concentrations.
        
- **Site-specific ECA documents**
    
    - Large projects like YTO40 often have multiple ECAs: one for air (generators), one for sewage works (cooling tower blowdown), and sometimes one for stormwater management.
