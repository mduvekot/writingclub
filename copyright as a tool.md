What kind of tool is "copyright"? What does it do, and to who or what does it do it?

Copyright regulates behaviour by granting/restricting rights.  The operations are *regulatory* operations; restrict, grant, permit. The operand is the change in behaviour of those who participate in creative markets; creators, consumers, rights holders.
